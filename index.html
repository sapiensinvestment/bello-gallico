Who We Are

A group that came together to build something bigger.

In October 2022, a group of people came together to pool their funds and invest them collectively. Today, in our fourth year, we are 20+ active members with total assets of around 86 million shillings, operating from our office at Tatitwe in Mbarara District.

Our philosophy is simple: what one cannot achieve alone, we achieve together — greater investment capacity, stronger social capital, steady growth toward financial freedom, and access to low-interest loans.
